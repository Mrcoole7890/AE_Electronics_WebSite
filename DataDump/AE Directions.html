Directions

Located in Williamsburg VA.

In the Jefferson Walk shopping complex

Near the intersection of Richmond Rd. and Ironbound Rd.

Between Mama Steve's House of Pancakes and the Goodyear Tire store



From the west (Richmond)

Take interstate 64 east to exit 234, route 199 toward Lightfoot. Take the third exit to route 60. At the bottom of the ramp turn right on route 60 east (Richmond Road). Follow route 60 east Richmond Road for approximately 3 miles into Williamsburg. As you enter Williamsburg, the road becomes un-divided with no median (only a center turn lane). Action Electronics is on the left side of the road, just past the third traffic light, across from the Discount Tire store. Turn left immediately after the third traffic light, or you can turn left at the third traffic light into the parking lot of KFC. There is a passthrough in the back right corner of the KFC parking lot into Mama Steve's parking lot. If you pass behind Mama Steve's Pancake house you will be pointed directly at the front door of Action Electronics, in the building on the other side of the parking lot.



From the east (Norfolk - VA Beach)

Take interstate 64 west to exit 238 (Camp Perry - Colonial Williamsburg). At the end of the ramp turn left toward Colonial Williamsburg on route 143. At the third traffic light turn right on route 132 toward Colonial Williamsburg. Follow route 132 to the next traffic light. Turn right at the light onto Bypass Road (Rt 60 Bypass west). Follow Bypass road for approximately 1.2 miles to Richmond Road (route 60 Business). Turn right on Richmond Road (route 60 west). Pass through the first traffic light at the turn on to Richmond Road, and pass through the second traffic light (High Street). Look for the Goodyear Tire Store on the right side of the road. Turn into the next parking lot immediately after passing the Goodyear store. Action electronics is on the side of the building on the right side of the parking lot.
